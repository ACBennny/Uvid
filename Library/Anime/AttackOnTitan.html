Attack on Titan

About

Attack on Titan is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. The story is set in a world where humanity is forced to live in cities surrounded by three enormous walls that protect them from gigantic man-eating humanoids referred to as Titans. The protagonist, Eren Yeager, vows to exterminate the Titans after they bring about the destruction of his hometown and the death of his mother.
