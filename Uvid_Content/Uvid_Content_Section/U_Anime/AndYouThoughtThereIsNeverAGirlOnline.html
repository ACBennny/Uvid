And You Thought There Is Never A Girl Online

About

And You Thought There Is Never a Girl Online is a romantic comedy anime series based on a light novel of the same name. It follows the adventures of a group of online gamers who form a school club and try to balance their gaming and real lives. The main character, Hideki Nishimura, is a high school student who plays a game called Legendary Age under the name Rusian. He is married in-game to a girl named Ako, who turns out to be his classmate and has trouble distinguishing reality from the game world.
